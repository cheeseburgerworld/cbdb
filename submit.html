Before your first review

→
Report, don't sell. "8oz, medium, good crust" tells more than "amazing." The goal is to be helpful.
→
Price it honestly. Pick the tier that matches what you actually paid.
→
That burger, that visit. Review what you actually ordered the day you went. Don't post a photo you found online.
→
Don't be a jerk. Be fair — don't tank a great burger over a slammed kitchen.
