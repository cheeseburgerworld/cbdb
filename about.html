About CBDB

The idea

A record of the world's burgers, written by people who actually ate them. Find the spot in your own neighborhood worth your time — and the one to seek out when you're in a city you don't know.

No ads. No algorithms. Just burgers.

Every other review platform answers to advertisers. Restaurants pay to boost their listing or bury a bad review. Feeds are tuned to keep you scrolling, not necessarily for your benefit. On those platforms, you are the product and your usage feeds a larger online advertising ecosystem. Here, we opt out of that. There's nothing to sell, and no one paying to change what you see.

How it works

Sign in, fill out a short form, and add a photo of the actual burger you ate. Your review posts to your own Bluesky profile and shows up here on the map and in the database.

The rating scale has four levels and every one matters. It works like the Michelin guide: the standard never bends to commercial pressure, so the rating means something. ⭐⭐⭐ Legendary is rare by design — overuse destroys it. A ㄨ Skip It is published without hesitation. A list that only surfaces praise isn't a guide. It's a marketing channel.
