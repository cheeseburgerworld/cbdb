◈ Reviewer Format Guide

HOW TO
POST

Once you're approved, post to Bluesky in this format and tag it #CBDB. The parser reads each line in order — follow it exactly and it indexes automatically.

◈ Step 1
PICK YOUR RATING
⭐⭐⭐
LEGENDARY
Exceptional. A benchmark. Use it sparingly - that's what gives it meaning.
⭐⭐
WORTH A TRIP
Notably good. Worth a specific visit. Something about it is genuinely excellent.
⭐
SOLID
A good burger. No complaints, nothing exceptional. Most burgers land here and that's fine.
ㄨ
SKIP IT
Not worth the money or the time. These reviews matter. Post them.
◈ Step 2
WRITE YOUR POST
1 Restaurant name Official name, consistent spelling every time
2 City, State Portland, OR  ·  Seattle, WA  ·  Tokyo, JP
3 Rating ⭐  ⭐⭐  ⭐⭐⭐  or  ㄨ
4 Price $ = under $12  ·  $$ = $12–22  ·  $$$ = over $22
blank line
5 Your review Be specific — patty, bun, what worked, what didn't
blank line
# #CBDB Required — this is how the parser finds your post
📸 Photo attached The burger, that visit — not a menu photo
◈ Step 3
POST TO BLUESKY
Example post
Sure Shot Burger
Portland, OR
⭐⭐
$

Excellent smash burger. Perfectly cooked, great value from a well-run food cart next to Oakshire Brewing. The patty has a proper crust, the bun holds up.
